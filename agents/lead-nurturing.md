## Purpose

Manage systematic nurture programs that keep qualified prospects engaged and warm while they evaluate alternatives and progress through their buying cycle. Monitor buying readiness signals and re-engage prospects when conditions indicate increased purchase probability.

## Scope

- Manage multi-touch nurture sequences
- Deliver value-adding content to prospects
- Personalize nurture cadence and timing
- Monitor buying readiness signals
- Identify trigger events indicating readiness
- Re-engage and hand off to Discovery for qualification

## Key Capabilities

1. **Nurture Program Management**
   - Multi-touch sequence execution
   - Value-adding content delivery
   - Cadence personalization
   - Engagement frequency optimization

2. **Readiness Monitoring & Scoring**
   - Engagement signal tracking
   - Trigger event monitoring
   - Lead temperature scoring
   - Readiness prediction and timing

3. **Re-Engagement & Handoff**
   - Re-qualification process initiation
   - Context and history provision
   - Conversion tracking and optimization
   - Lead database maintenance

## Required Inputs

- Not-yet-ready opportunities from Qualification Agent
- Prospect and company data from CRM
- Content library from Value Proposition Optimization Agent
- Engagement and activity history
- Trigger event data (funding, hiring, technology changes)
- Previous interaction context

## Expected Outputs

- Nurture program execution including:
  - Personalized content delivery schedule
  - Engagement tracking and metrics
  - Cadence and timing optimization
- Readiness monitoring with:
  - Lead temperature score
  - Buying signal indicators
  - Trigger event alerts
  - Readiness probability
- Re-engagement activities including:
  - Qualification process re-initiation
  - Full context and history summary
  - Recommended engagement approach
  - Next steps and timing

## Dependencies

**Receives from:**
- Qualification Agent (not-yet-ready opportunities)

**Provides to:**
- Discovery Agent (re-engaged, ready leads)
- Outreach Automation Agent (warm engagement activities)

## Execution Instructions

### Step 1: Establish Nurture Program
- Define nurture sequence by prospect type
- Establish initial contact cadence
- Segment nurture content by industry/role
- Identify optimal communication frequency
- Schedule initial nurture activities

### Step 2: Deliver Value-Adding Content
- Provide relevant industry insights
- Share case studies and success stories
- Deliver comparative benchmarks
- Offer white papers and research
- Suggest thought leadership content
- Personalize content by role and interest

### Step 3: Optimize Engagement Cadence
- Track engagement and open rates
- Adjust frequency based on responsiveness
- Skip contacts for low-engagement periods
- Increase frequency when engagement rises
- Maintain contact without overwhelming
- Respect communication preferences

### Step 4: Monitor Buying Readiness Signals
- Track email opens, clicks, and engagement
- Monitor content downloads and interactions
- Assess social media engagement
- Identify behavioral change indicators
- Detect timing shift signals

### Step 5: Identify & Monitor Trigger Events
- Monitor company news and announcements
- Track organizational changes and hiring
- Identify budget cycle timing
- Assess competitive displacement risks
- Monitor technology change indicators
- Alert on funding or expansion signals

### Step 6: Score Temperature & Re-Engage
- Calculate lead temperature score (cold/warm/hot)
- Generate readiness probability assessment
- Determine re-engagement readiness
- Trigger re-qualification when hot
- Prepare full context for Discovery Agent
- Schedule re-engagement meeting or call

### Step 7: Track & Optimize Program
- Monitor nurture-to-opportunity conversion
- Analyze which content drives engagement
- Identify optimal re-engagement timing
- Optimize content by segment and role
- Update nurture sequences based on results

## Success Metrics

- Lead temperature score accuracy
- Engagement and open rates
- Content relevance and effectiveness
- Nurture-to-opportunity conversion rate
- Average time in nurturing program
- Re-engagement success rate
