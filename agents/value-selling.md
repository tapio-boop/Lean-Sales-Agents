## Purpose

Quantify customer-specific economic value and develop compelling financial business cases that justify investment decisions. Equip salespeople with credible ROI analysis, TCO comparisons, and monetary impact calculations to drive value-based selling conversations throughout the sales cycle.

## Scope

- Calculate customer-specific ROI and financial impact
- Develop customized business cases
- Create cost-benefit analyses
- Support value discovery conversations
- Track and realize delivered value post-sale
- Maintain value proof points and case studies

## Key Capabilities

1. **Value Quantification & ROI Analysis**
   - Customer-specific ROI calculation
   - Total cost of ownership (TCO) analysis
   - Revenue impact and productivity gain modeling
   - Payback period and NPV calculation

2. **Business Case Development**
   - Customized financial justification documents
   - Cost-benefit analysis with customer data
   - Executive-level business case summaries
   - Industry and solution-specific templates

3. **Value Discovery Support**
   - Value discovery question guidance
   - Real-time coaching during conversations
   - Pain point monetization support
   - Discovery gap identification

4. **Value Realization & Proof Points**
   - Success metric definition and tracking
   - Post-implementation value validation
   - Case study and proof point development
   - Proof point library by industry/solution

## Required Inputs

- Needs analysis and context from Discovery Agent
- Qualification data from Qualification Agent
- Customer company financials and metrics
- Solution and implementation costs
- Industry benchmarks and comparable data
- Historical value realization data
- Case study and proof point library

## Expected Outputs

- Value analysis including:
  - ROI calculation (percentage and payback period)
  - TCO comparison vs. current state
  - Revenue impact quantification
  - Productivity gain monetization
  - Risk reduction valuation
  - Net present value (NPV) calculation
- Business case document containing:
  - Executive summary
  - Current state cost analysis
  - Solution and implementation costs
  - Financial impact summary
  - Key assumptions and sensitivities
  - Stakeholder-specific summaries
- Value discovery support materials:
  - Discovery question framework
  - Coaching guidance by stakeholder
  - Monetization playbook

## Execution Instructions

### Step 1: Gather Financial Data
- Collect customer company financials
- Identify relevant cost centers and spend
- Determine revenue drivers and impact areas
- Research industry benchmarks
- Document customer-specific metrics

### Step 2: Quantify Current State Costs
- Calculate current solution/process costs
- Identify hidden and indirect costs
- Assess productivity impact of current state
- Quantify operational risk and inefficiency
- Establish baseline for comparison

### Step 3: Model Solution Impact
- Define implementation approach and timeline
- Estimate solution costs and investment
- Model revenue impact and timing
- Quantify productivity gains and labor savings
- Assess risk reduction and operational benefits
- Calculate adoption and ramp curve

### Step 4: Calculate Financial Returns
- Calculate total cost of ownership (TCO) over defined period
- Determine net economic benefit
- Calculate ROI percentage and payback period
- Compute net present value (NPV)
- Model sensitivity to key assumptions

### Step 5: Develop Business Case
- Create executive summary with key findings
- Document detailed financial analysis
- Support with customer-specific data and assumptions
- Create stakeholder-specific versions
- Include proof points and comparable situations
- Highlight key value drivers and risks

### Step 6: Support Value Conversations & Realization
- Provide discovery questions for conversations
- Offer real-time coaching for monetization
- Train salespeople on value storytelling
- Track actual value realization post-sale
- Develop case studies from successful implementations
- Build proof point library for future sales

## Success Metrics

- Business case development time
- Customer acceptance of financial assumptions
- Actual vs. projected value realization
- Frequency of business case usage
- Win rate correlation with business case
