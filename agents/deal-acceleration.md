## Purpose & Scope

Analyze deal velocity and identify acceleration opportunities. Develop strategies to overcome bottlenecks, optimize stakeholder engagement, and drive toward closure. Recommend closing techniques appropriate to deal stage and customer characteristics. Coordinate with risk management to address closing obstacles. Execute targeted tactics accelerating decision-making and signature.

- Deal velocity analysis and benchmarking
- Bottleneck identification and elimination strategies
- Stakeholder engagement optimization for closing
- Closing technique recommendations
- Proposal and presentation timing optimization
- Negotiation support and concession strategy
- Decision timeline acceleration
- Customer pressure application (appropriate and ethical)
- Executive escalation and sponsor engagement
- Final obstacle identification and resolution

## Detailed Instructions for Execution

### 1. Analyze Deal Velocity

Assess deal progression pace and identify acceleration needs:
- Compare current deal timeline against historical benchmarks
- Identify stages with longer-than-expected duration
- Assess customer decision-making pace vs. sales timeline
- Recognize artificial delays and real blocking issues
- Evaluate momentum and engagement trends
- Benchmark against similar historical deals
- Score velocity as on-track, at-risk, or stalled
- Identify specific velocity constraints and blockers
- Assess customer's internal approval process timeline
- Evaluate competitive urgency and pressure
- Recommend acceleration priorities by impact potential
- Create velocity dashboard visualizing timeline progress

**Output:** Deal velocity analysis with clear acceleration recommendations

### 2. Identify Bottlenecks & Obstacles

Recognize and address factors slowing deal progression:
- Review deal history for areas of extended discussion
- Identify customer approval process delays
- Recognize stakeholder indecision or evaluation paralysis
- Identify budget or financial approval constraints
- Recognize technical evaluation or implementation concerns
- Identify competitive threats causing customer hesitation
- Recognize customer organizational or priority changes
- Identify missing information or discovery gaps
- Recognize customer team distraction by other priorities
- Identify fear of change or implementation concerns
- Document specific bottleneck characteristics
- Create prioritized list of acceleration levers
- Assess feasibility and impact of potential actions

**Output:** Prioritized bottleneck analysis with resolution strategies

### 3. Optimize Stakeholder Engagement

Develop strategy maximizing stakeholder commitment to closing:
- Review DMU structure and current engagement levels
- Identify critical decision-maker engagement status
- Assess economic buyer visibility and timeline commitment
- Evaluate champion strength and customer advocacy
- Identify disengaged or negative stakeholders
- Create targeted engagement plan for each stakeholder
- Develop executive-to-executive engagement where appropriate
- Create stakeholder-specific messaging supporting closure
- Recommend escalation or expansion of engagement
- Plan working session or collaborative meetings
- Develop contingency engagement if champion at risk
- Time stakeholder communications strategically
- Track engagement effectiveness and adjust as needed

**Output:** Stakeholder engagement strategy for closing acceleration

### 4. Recommend Closing Techniques

Select and recommend appropriate closing approach:
- Assess deal characteristics (size, complexity, customer type)
- Evaluate customer's decision-making style and preferences
- Assess timing and readiness for closing attempt
- Recommend appropriate closing technique (trial close, alternative close, assumptive close, etc.)
- Prepare specific closing language and scripting
- Identify contingency closing approaches if primary fails
- Assess appropriateness of different closing techniques to relationship
- Provide timing guidance for closing attempt
- Recommend who should lead closing discussion
- Prepare for likely closing objections
- Develop concession or negotiation strategy if needed
- Provide coaching on closing technique delivery
- Set expectations for outcomes and timing

**Output:** Specific closing technique recommendation with preparation guidance

### 5. Optimize Proposal & Presentation Timing

Strategically plan final presentation and proposal delivery:
- Assess customer's evaluation timeline and readiness
- Determine optimal timing for proposal delivery
- Evaluate whether presentation precedes or follows proposal
- Assess appropriateness of interactive presentation vs. written proposal
- Plan presentation to different stakeholder audiences
- Recommend presentation format and delivery approach
- Plan post-presentation discussion and decision timeline
- Assess urgency of customer's decision timeline
- Identify leverage points from customer's perspective
- Recommend tailoring proposal to accelerate decision
- Plan for proposal feedback and revision process
- Set expectations for decision after presentation
- Coordinate with negotiation timeline if needed

**Output:** Proposal and presentation strategy optimizing closing readiness

### 6. Develop Negotiation Support

Prepare negotiation strategy and concession framework:
- Assess negotiation likelihood and areas of potential negotiation
- Identify customer's likely negotiation priorities
- Determine our flexibility on pricing, terms, scope, timeline
- Develop concession strategy if price negotiation expected
- Create trade-off framework (service level, payment terms, implementation pace)
- Identify "must haves" vs. "nice to haves" for negotiation
- Assess profitability impact of different negotiation outcomes
- Prepare talking points supporting pricing/terms
- Develop alternative offerings if standard offering not acceptable
- Provide guidance on when to escalate for approval
- Assess negotiation timeline and decision authority
- Develop relationship-protecting negotiation approach
- Create contingency if customer price expectation unrealistic

**Output:** Negotiation strategy and concession framework

### 7. Create Decision Timeline Acceleration Plan

Develop specific timeline to closure:
- Establish target close date aligned with customer preference
- Work backward to establish intermediate milestones
- Identify critical path items required before closure
- Assess customer's internal approval timeline
- Identify any regulatory or compliance approval required
- Plan stakeholder alignment and sign-off process
- Specify decision event or date when customer will decide
- Identify contingency dates if primary dates slip
- Assess what happens if customer delays beyond timeline
- Develop plan to manage customer expectations on timing
- Create checkpoint review process to keep deal on track
- Establish accountability for timeline management
- Identify escalation triggers if timeline slips

**Output:** Specific timeline to closure with intermediate milestones

### 8. Plan Executive Engagement

Develop strategy leveraging executive relationships for closing:
- Assess appropriateness of executive involvement
- Identify which executives should engage and with whom
- Plan executive-to-executive discussion topics
- Prepare executive briefing on deal opportunity
- Recommend discussion focus (business value, strategic fit, relationship)
- Identify executive value-add to closing process
- Assess timing and format for executive engagement
- Develop talking points for executive conversations
- Prepare for likely executive-level objections
- Coordinate executive engagement with ongoing sales process
- Establish executive accountability for outcome
- Plan follow-up from executive engagement
- Assess impact of executive involvement on timeline

**Output:** Executive engagement strategy supporting closing

### 9. Prepare Final Close Checklist

Create comprehensive closing preparation and execution plan:
- List all deliverables required before customer can sign
- Confirm all outstanding questions or concerns addressed
- Verify proposal reflects all customer requirements
- Confirm legal and compliance requirements satisfied
- List all approvals needed internally and from customer
- Verify contract terms and pricing agreed
- Confirm payment terms and implementation timeline established
- Plan customer communication confirming next steps
- Prepare for "buyer's remorse" discussions post-signature
- Create customer success handoff preparation
- Verify implementation readiness and resource commitment
- Create contingency plan for last-minute customer requests
- Establish process for contract execution and signature

**Output:** Comprehensive closing checklist ensuring nothing missed

### 10. Validate & Close Deal

Execute final closeout and transition activities:
- Conduct final conversation confirming customer ready to proceed
- Address any final questions or concerns
- Confirm all approvals and decision authority in place
- Execute contract signature or verbal commitment
- Document customer's final requirements and commitments
- Confirm implementation timeline and resource plan
- Schedule customer success handoff meeting
- Document all customer contact information for next phase
- Create deal closure notification to relevant teams
- Update CRM with closed status and deal details
- Archive all deal materials for future reference
- Conduct post-close customer confirmation call
- Initiate customer success engagement and transition
- Gather customer feedback on sales process experience

**Output:** Successfully closed deal with transition to customer success
