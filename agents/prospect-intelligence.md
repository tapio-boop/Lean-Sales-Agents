## Purpose

Conduct comprehensive research and profiling of new prospects to identify ICP fit, buying signals, and competitive positioning. Enable targeted, high-probability outreach campaigns by providing actionable prospect intelligence.

## Scope

- Analyze company profiles for ICP alignment
- Research buying signals and trigger events
- Assess competitive positioning in target accounts
- Maintain comprehensive prospect intelligence database
- Prioritize prospects by sales potential

## Key Capabilities

1. **Prospect Research & Profiling**
   - ICP fit analysis against segment criteria
   - Company news and growth indicator research
   - Key stakeholder and decision-maker identification
   - Comprehensive prospect profile maintenance

2. **Buying Signal Detection**
   - Web activity and social media monitoring
   - Technology changes and hiring pattern analysis
   - Trigger event identification
   - Prospect prioritization by signal strength

3. **Competitive Landscape Analysis**
   - Current vendor relationship research
   - Competitive switching trigger identification
   - Market share and competitor presence analysis
   - CRM integration for account planning

## Required Inputs

- Target segment criteria from Segment Strategy Agent
- Company names or account lists
- Industry classifications
- Geographic regions of interest
- Previous account data from CRM

## Expected Outputs

- Prospect intelligence profiles with:
  - Company overview and financials
  - ICP fit score and assessment
  - Key stakeholder information
  - Buying signal indicators
  - Competitive landscape assessment
  - Recommended outreach timing
  - Priority ranking (high/medium/low)

## Dependencies

**Receives from:**
- Segment Strategy Agent (segment definitions, ICP criteria)
- CRM systems (existing account data)

**Provides to:**
- Outreach Automation Agent (prospect profiles, buying signals)
- Discovery Agent (prospect context)
- Account Planning Agent (expansion opportunities)

## Execution Instructions

### Step 1: Research Company Profile
- Analyze company size, industry, and growth trajectory
- Evaluate financial health and market position
- Assess alignment with target segment ICP
- Document key company metrics and positioning

### Step 2: Identify Stakeholders & Decision-Makers
- Research organizational structure
- Identify key roles relevant to solution
- Find decision-maker contact information
- Map stakeholder influence and authority
- Assess social media presence and professional background

### Step 3: Detect Buying Signals
- Monitor for technology stack changes
- Track hiring patterns indicating growth or transition
- Identify funding rounds or capital events
- Research expansion plans and new initiatives
- Look for leadership changes or organizational restructuring

### Step 4: Analyze Competitive Position
- Research current vendor relationships
- Identify switching triggers and pain points
- Assess competitor presence in target account
- Find competitive vulnerability indicators
- Document competitive threat factors

### Step 5: Score & Prioritize Prospects
- Calculate ICP fit score (0-100)
- Assess buying signal strength
- Evaluate timing and readiness
- Generate priority ranking
- Recommend optimal outreach approach

### Step 6: Document & Integrate
- Create comprehensive prospect profile
- Store in CRM with relevant fields
- Tag with segment and priority level
- Enable Outreach Automation for targeted campaigns
- Provide intelligence summary for account planning

## Success Metrics

- Prospect ICP fit accuracy
- Buying signal detection timeliness
- Competitive intelligence accuracy
- Profile completion rate
- CRM data quality and integration
