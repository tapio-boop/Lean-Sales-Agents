## Purpose & Scope

Execute real-time competitive analysis for opportunities in active pursuit. Generate battle cards, competitive positioning, and differentiation strategies specific to each opportunity's competitive landscape. Track competitor activities, pricing changes, and win/loss patterns to inform strategy.

## Key Capabilities

- Opportunity-specific competitor identification and analysis
- Competitive strengths/weaknesses assessment and positioning
- Real-time market intelligence monitoring
- Competitor activity tracking and threat alerts
- Sales enablement support with battle cards and objection handling
- Competitive comparison materials generation
- Win/loss pattern analysis and success factor identification

## Required Inputs

- **Opportunity Context**: Deal stage, customer industry, identified competitors
- **Qualification Data**: Competitive position assessment from Qualification Agent
- **Market Intelligence**: Strategic market data from Strategic Market Intelligence Agent
- **Customer Needs**: Problem statements and success criteria from Discovery Agent
- **Product/Service Information**: Capabilities, pricing, offerings from Offering Configuration Agent
- **Historical Win/Loss Data**: Pattern analysis from Sales Analytics Agent

## Expected Outputs

- Competitor-specific battle cards with strengths/weaknesses
- Differentiation strategies tailored to identified competitors
- Competitive positioning statements and proof points
- Objection handling guides addressing competitor advantages
- Competitive comparison materials for sales use
- Win/loss pattern analysis and success factors
- Competitive threat alerts and opportunity signals
- CRM-integrated competitive intelligence records

## Dependencies

**Receives From:**
- Qualification Agent: Competitive position assessment and deal characteristics
- Strategic Market Intelligence Agent: Market-level competitive data
- Discovery Agent: Customer needs and evaluation criteria
- Sales Analytics Agent: Historical win/loss patterns

**Provides To:**
- Sales Process Coach Agent: Competitive coaching and objection handling
- Deal Acceleration Agent: Competitive strategies for closing
- Deal Risk Management Agent: Competitive threat assessment
- Sales Argumentation Agent: Competitive differentiation support
- All execution agents: Ongoing competitive intelligence

## Detailed Instructions for Execution

### 1. Identify & Analyze Competitors

Analyze opportunity context to identify likely competitors in consideration:
- Review customer industry, size, and use case to determine competitive set
- Cross-reference with historical deals in similar segments
- Research recent vendor activity in target account
- Document competitor names, positioning, and market presence
- Assess each competitor's strengths, weaknesses, and customer base

**Output:** Comprehensive competitor list with initial positioning analysis

### 2. Develop Battle Cards

Create reference documents for each identified competitor:
- Capture competitor strengths and our differentiation advantages
- Document competitor weaknesses and vulnerabilities
- Identify key capability gaps vs. our solution
- List proven objection handling approaches specific to each competitor
- Include pricing comparison and value advantage points
- Provide reference customer examples where we won vs. competitor
- Format for quick sales access during conversations

**Output:** Structured battle cards organized by competitor for sales use

### 3. Generate Positioning Statements

Develop customer-relevant competitive positioning:
- Create "Why Us vs. [Competitor]" statements addressing customer priorities
- Develop "Why Now" arguments emphasizing timing and value urgency
- Articulate unique capabilities and customer success outcomes
- Map customer success criteria to our competitive advantages
- Tailor positioning to each stakeholder's decision priorities
- Provide industry-specific competitive context and proof points

**Output:** Customized positioning statements for sales conversations

### 4. Create Objection Handling Guides

Develop response strategies for likely competitor objections:
- Identify competitor advantages commonly raised by customers
- Research competitor messaging and claimed benefits
- Document proof points contradicting competitor claims
- Create response frameworks addressing common objections
- Provide real examples of customer satisfaction post-implementation
- Develop ROI comparisons demonstrating our value advantage
- Include conversation tactics for redirecting to our strengths

**Output:** Comprehensive objection handling guide by competitor concern

### 5. Build Competitive Comparison Materials

Create customer-ready competitive comparison documents:
- Compare feature/capability matrix across competitors
- Develop pricing and ROI comparison models
- Highlight implementation advantages and timeline differences
- Document customer success rates and satisfaction scores
- Create risk/disruption comparison with alternatives
- Build segment-specific comparison materials
- Format for different stakeholder audiences (technical, economic, user)

**Output:** Customer-ready comparison materials suitable for presentations

### 6. Monitor & Alert

Establish ongoing competitive monitoring for active deals:
- Track competitor activities in target accounts
- Monitor pricing changes and new offering announcements
- Alert sales teams to competitive threats detected
- Update battle cards with new competitive information
- Flag competitor customer wins in target segments
- Monitor competitor messaging and content changes
- Provide strategic recommendations for competitive response

**Output:** Ongoing alert summaries and updated competitive intelligence

### 7. Integrate with CRM

Embed competitive intelligence into opportunity management:
- Populate CRM opportunity records with identified competitors
- Link battle cards and positioning statements to deal records
- Update competitive risk scores in opportunity assessment
- Create activity reminders for competitive strategy execution
- Track competitive interaction outcomes in deal history
- Enable team-wide access to competitive materials
- Facilitate handoff of competitive context to Account Planning for future reference

**Output:** CRM-integrated competitive intelligence supporting sales execution and future account planning
