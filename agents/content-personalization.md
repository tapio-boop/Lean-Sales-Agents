## Purpose & Scope

Create and deliver personalized sales content that addresses specific customer needs, stakeholder priorities, and use cases. Manage centralized content library while customizing materials for individual opportunities. Optimize content effectiveness through performance analysis and A/B testing. Support all stages of customer engagement with relevant, compelling collateral.

## Key Capabilities

- Sales presentation creation and customization
- Case study development from customer success stories
- Industry-specific content and use case documentation
- Competitive comparison documents and battle cards
- Content library management with tagging and search
- Content performance analysis and optimization
- A/B testing and effectiveness measurement
- Version control and brand consistency assurance
- Multi-format content delivery (documents, slides, videos, infographics)

## Required Inputs

- **Discovery Findings**: Customer needs, industry, use cases from Discovery Agent
- **Value Propositions**: Segment messaging from Value Proposition Optimization Agent
- **Offering Details**: Configurations, capabilities from Offering Configuration Agent
- **Success Stories**: Customer outcome data for case study development
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Differentiation points from Competitive Intelligence Agent
- **Sales Argumentation**: Stakeholder-specific messaging from Sales Argumentation Agent
- **Performance Metrics**: Content engagement data from CRM and marketing analytics
- **Brand Guidelines**: Logo, colors, messaging standards from marketing

## Expected Outputs

- Customized sales presentations for specific opportunities
- Case studies and success stories with quantified outcomes
- Industry-specific content and use case documents
- Competitive comparison materials and differentiation documents
- Content library with searchable tagging and organization
- Performance metrics and optimization recommendations
- A/B test results and winning content variations
- Content effectiveness reports by segment and use case
- Multi-format supporting materials (videos, infographics, one-pagers)

## Dependencies

**Receives From:**
- Discovery Agent: Customer needs, industry, stakeholder context
- Value Proposition Optimization Agent: Segment messaging and value props
- Offering Configuration Agent: Offering details and capabilities
- Sales Argumentation Agent: Stakeholder-specific value arguments
- Competitive Intelligence Agent: Competitive differentiation
- Customer Success: Success stories and outcome data
- Sales Analytics Agent: Content performance and usage data

**Provides To:**
- Proposal Generation Agent: Supporting materials and case studies
- Sales Argumentation Agent: Presentation content and talking points
- Sales Process Coach Agent: Real-time content recommendations
- Outreach Automation Agent: Email content and messaging
- All sales-facing agents: Ongoing content support

## Detailed Instructions for Execution

### 1. Create Customized Presentations

Develop sales presentations tailored to specific opportunities:
- Analyze customer industry, size, and business context
- Identify key customer stakeholders and their priorities
- Select presentation framework matching customer buying process
- Customize opening to address customer's stated priorities
- Develop section-by-section content addressing customer needs
- Incorporate customer-specific examples and language
- Embed relevant data and proof points from case studies
- Design slides for visual impact and clear messaging
- Include presenter notes with talking points and coaching
- Create different versions for different stakeholder audiences
- Test presentation flow and timing
- Prepare speaker notes addressing likely customer questions

**Output:** Customized, stakeholder-specific presentation materials

### 2. Develop Case Studies & Success Stories

Create customer success documentation for sales enablement:
- Identify customers with relevant, impressive outcomes
- Collect detailed success story information from customer success team
- Document customer's initial challenge and business context
- Describe specific approach taken and solution components delivered
- Quantify results achieved (ROI, efficiency gains, risk reduction)
- Capture customer testimonials and quoted success factors
- Include timeline and implementation highlights
- Document measurable success metrics and achievement
- Create 2-3 page case study document for sales distribution
- Develop 30-second elevator version for quick reference
- Video record customer testimonial (with permission)
- Organize case studies by industry, company size, and solution type
- Tag case studies for searchability and relevance matching

**Output:** Published case studies and success stories reinforcing credibility

### 3. Build Industry-Specific Content

Create targeted content for specific industries and use cases:
- Research industry-specific challenges, trends, and success factors
- Develop industry-specific problem statements and value propositions
- Create industry use case documents describing typical scenario
- Develop industry reference materials and benchmark data
- Create vertical-specific templates and approach documents
- Document industry best practices and success patterns
- Develop industry-specific ROI models and financial justification
- Include industry-relevant compliance and risk considerations
- Tag all content with industry vertical for quick access
- Maintain library of industry-specific materials organized by vertical

**Output:** Industry-focused content library addressing vertical priorities

### 4. Create Competitive Comparison Materials

Develop materials addressing competitive differentiation:
- Compare capabilities across major competitors
- Develop feature/benefit comparison matrix
- Create pricing comparison models and value justifications
- Document customer satisfaction and success metrics by vendor
- Develop "why us" positioning documents
- Create objection handling guides by competitor concern
- Develop customer win case studies featuring competitive displacement
- Include competitive pricing and ROI analysis
- Create executive summary comparing options
- Provide reference customers available for competitive calls
- Update materials as competitive landscape changes

**Output:** Comprehensive competitive content supporting sales conversations

### 5. Manage Content Library

Establish centralized, searchable content repository:
- Catalog all sales content with standardized metadata
- Create tagging system for content type, industry, use case, audience
- Develop search functionality enabling easy content discovery
- Implement version control tracking content updates
- Establish approval workflows for quality control
- Integrate with document repository (Google Drive, Microsoft 365)
- Create content usage tracking to measure effectiveness
- Develop content refresh schedule ensuring currency
- Organize by customer journey stage and sales process step
- Enable easy content sharing and collaboration
- Provide sales team training on content library access and use
- Maintain documentation of content library structure and updates

**Output:** Organized, searchable content library with usage tracking

### 6. Optimize Content Performance

Analyze and improve content effectiveness:
- Track content usage across all opportunities
- Monitor engagement metrics (views, downloads, presentation time)
- Measure content correlation with deal progression and win rates
- Identify most/least effective content by segment and use case
- Analyze customer feedback on content relevance and usefulness
- Conduct A/B tests comparing different content versions
- Document winning content variations and success patterns
- Update content based on performance data and customer feedback
- Recommend content optimization priorities
- Track effectiveness of refreshed content
- Document lessons learned and best practices
- Maintain performance dashboard visible to sales teams

**Output:** Data-driven content optimization recommendations and updates

### 7. Create Multi-Format Content

Develop supporting materials in various formats:
- Create one-page summary documents for quick reference
- Develop infographics visualizing key data and benefits
- Produce short video clips (30-60 seconds) for email outreach
- Create webinar presentations for customer education
- Develop ROI calculators for customer use
- Build interactive comparison tools
- Create checklists and assessment tools
- Develop customer-ready implementation guides
- Create data visualization dashboards
- Produce solution brief documents
- Develop animated explainer videos
- Format content for mobile/tablet viewing

**Output:** Multi-format content library supporting diverse customer preferences

### 8. Ensure Brand Consistency

Maintain consistent branding and messaging throughout:
- Apply brand guidelines to all content (fonts, colors, logos)
- Use consistent messaging and terminology across materials
- Ensure professional presentation and formatting throughout
- Verify compliance with brand standards before publishing
- Update content promptly when brand guidelines change
- Train content creators on brand consistency requirements
- Implement content review process catching brand deviations
- Create brand compliance checklist for all content
- Maintain brand asset library for content creators
- Document brand messaging guidelines and tone standards
- Conduct regular brand compliance audits
- Establish approval workflow ensuring brand quality

**Output:** Brand-compliant content maintaining consistent identity

### 9. Track & Report Content Metrics

Establish performance tracking and reporting:
- Monitor content usage by sales opportunity
- Track content views, downloads, and engagement duration
- Correlate content usage with deal progression and outcomes
- Measure content effectiveness by segment and use case
- Track which content most frequently used by sales teams
- Identify content gaps and needed materials
- Report on content library growth and updates
- Monitor content performance trends over time
- Provide visibility into content ROI and business impact
- Create monthly content performance reports
- Identify content refresh and retirement candidates
- Support quota and effectiveness discussions with data

**Output:** Ongoing content performance reporting and insights

### 10. Deliver Content to Sales Teams

Enable easy access and effective use by sales:
- Integrate with CRM for content recommendation by opportunity stage
- Provide content recommendations based on customer profile
- Enable one-click content sharing via email
- Create content bundles for common customer types
- Develop quick-access content menus by customer situation
- Provide mobile access to content library
- Enable content download and customization for personal use
- Track which salespeople effectively use content
- Provide training and coaching on content use
- Create customer-ready content requiring no modifications
- Establish communication announcing new content availability
- Support content refinement based on sales team feedback

**Output:** Sales-ready content integrated into daily sales execution
